Expandable Groups View Docs

expandableGroups works on the concept of starting out with rows already collapsed. It basically shuffles items in the grid by removing rows that are collapsed out. So the grid just renders what is there. Expanding the row will then put those rows back in.

You can use the option of hideEmptyGroups to have expandableGroups automatically remove groups that have no children.
